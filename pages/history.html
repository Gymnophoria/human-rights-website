History Leading Up To the UDHR

  • 1215

    Magna Carta

    After revision, a civil rights bill, the Magna Carta, was passed into law. This enforced that rulers would follow the same rules as the people they were ruling.

  • July 4, 1776

    US Declaration of Independence Ratified

    The United States Declaration of Independence was signed and ratified, allowing Americans to escape the high taxes of Britain, also giving human rights to many.

  • August 26, 1789

    French Declaration of the Rights of Man

    Inspiring the French Revolution, French civilians created a document declaring the rights of all men, wanting safety and protection.

  • January 1, 1863

    Emancipation Proclamation

    Under the order of Abraham Lincoln, the Emancipation Proclamation freed all slaves from the union. Segregation began across the United States.

  • 1939 to 1945

    World War II

    World War Two begins and ends, during which millions of jews and others are killed in concentration camps. This rises much awareness of the bad that could be done by anyone with the wrong ideas.
