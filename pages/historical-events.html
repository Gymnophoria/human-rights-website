Historical Events Related to Human Rights

  • 1215 A.D.

    Magna Carta Created

    The Magna Carta is created, enforcing that rulers must follow the rules they enforce upon their people, meaning less human rights violations from the rulers themselves.

  • December 16, 1689

    English Bill of Rights Created

    The English Bill of Rights enforced certain basic civil rights.

  • August 26, 1789

    French Declaration of the Rights of Man

    The French Declaration of the Rights of Man was created, giving certain human rights to all people

  • December 15, 1791

    US Bill of Rights Ratified

    The United States Bill of Rights is ratified, adding to the list of human and civil rights that United States citizens get.

  • 1919

    Nazi Party Gains Traction

    The Nazi party starts to gain traction, spreading their influential and horrible ways around.
