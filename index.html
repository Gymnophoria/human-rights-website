Definition of Human and Civil Rights

Human Rights are given to everyone and apply everywhere. Human Rights are generally what you need to live in the modern world, such as the right to live, not be enslaved, be able to move, and many more. Civil Rights are formed by countries and can change from country to country. Civil Rights are asked for by the citizens for different reasons. Human Rights and Civil Rights are meant to protect people from others or help people through life.
